Frequently Asked Questions

🎓 Is this curriculum really free?

Yes! OpenSystemClasses is completely free and open-source. You can use, modify, and share our materials without any restrictions. The curriculum is licensed under the MIT License.

👩‍🎓 What age group is this for?

Our curriculum is designed for students aged 8-14. However, the materials can be adapted for older beginners or younger students with some guidance. No prior programming experience is required!

💻 What hardware do I need?

For the basic Python classes, you only need a computer. For the hardware classes, you'll need a Raspberry Pi Pico and some basic electronic components. Check our Hardware Guide for a complete list.

👨‍🏫 Can I use this in my classroom?

Absolutely! Our curriculum is designed for both individual learners and classroom use. We provide complete teacher materials including lesson plans, solutions, and assessment tools.

📚 How long does each class take?

Each class is designed to take approximately 4-6 weeks, with 1-2 hours of instruction per week. However, the pace can be adjusted based on your students' needs and available time.

🤖 Do I need programming experience?

No prior programming experience is required for students or educators! Our curriculum starts from absolute basics and provides comprehensive teacher guides.
